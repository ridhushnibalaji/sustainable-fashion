Making Better Choices for Our Planet

What is Sustainable Fashion?

Sustainable fashion is clothing that's designed, manufactured, and distributed in a way that is environmentally friendly and responsible. The movement is about creating a system for clothing that can work long-term without wasting resources or abusing people.

Pillars

  • Eco-friendly materials (organic cotton, hemp, recycled fabrics)
  • Designed for durability
  • Fair, safe working conditions

What is Fast Fashion?

The opposite of sustainable fashion.

  • Cheap materials
  • Produced quickly (microtrends)
  • Designed to be worn briefly

Sustainable Alternatives

1. Thrifting & Secondhand Shopping

Thrifting or buying secondhand is one of the best ways to reduce your fashion footprint. It lengthens the lifecycle of clothing and also reduces the need for more production.

  • Thrift stores
  • Online platforms -- Depop, Poshmark, ThredUp
  • Clothing swaps with friends
  • Pop-ups
  • Vintage stores

2. Sustainable Brands

Certain brands prioritize sustainability. Look for ones that do the following.

  • Use organic or recycled materials
  • Pay fair wages to workers
  • Have repair services or take-back programs

3. Sustainable Materials

Best Choices

  • Organic cotton
  • Hemp
  • Linen
  • Tencel/Lyocell
  • Recycled polyester

Avoid When You Can

  • Conventional cotton
  • Polyester
  • Nylon
  • Acrylic
  • Rayon

4. Renting/Borrowing

If you have a special occasion and only need to wear something once, you can do the following.

  • Rent from clothing rental services
  • Borrow from friends/family
  • Share with others

Practical Tips for Sustainable Fashion

Buy Less, Choose Well

Spend your money on fewer, but high-quality pieces that you'll wear for a long time. Quality over quantity.

Repair and Upcycle

Learn some basic sewing skills to repair holes and tears. You can turn old clothes into new pieces.

Think Before You Buy

Ask yourself how often you'll wear something before you buy it. Try not to buy things off impulse or microtrends.

So be sustainable when you can!

Sustainable fashion is a lot more than just sustainable clothes. It's a movement towards a better future for our planet and our people.

Stay conscious about the effects of your choices, and know the power you have!
